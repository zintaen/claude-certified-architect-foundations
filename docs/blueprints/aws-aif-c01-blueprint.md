## Learning objectives (outline, own words)

### Fundamentals of AI and ML

- Distinguish AI, ML, and related terms at a practitioner level
- Recognize common problem types suited to ML vs rules

### Fundamentals of GenAI

- Describe foundation models and generative use cases
- Reason about prompts, tokens, and basic cost/latency tradeoffs

### Applications of Foundation Models

- Match business needs to FM application patterns
- Identify when RAG, fine-tuning, or prompt-only approaches fit

### Guidelines for Responsible AI

- Apply fairness, transparency, and human oversight concepts
- Spot misuse and hallucination risks in GenAI apps

### Security, Compliance, and Governance for AI Solutions

- Recognize data protection and access-control concerns for AI workloads
- Map governance checkpoints to solution lifecycle stages
