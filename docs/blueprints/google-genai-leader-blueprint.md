## Learning objectives (outline, own words)

### Generative AI concepts for leaders

- Explain foundation models, prompts, and limitations without requiring coding depth

### Value and use-case selection

- Prioritize GenAI opportunities by business value and feasibility

### Responsible AI and risk

- Identify privacy, bias, hallucination, and brand risks for GenAI programs

### Data and grounding readiness

- Recognize when enterprise data readiness enables or blocks GenAI apps

### Operating model and change

- Describe skills, governance, and rollout checkpoints leaders should fund
